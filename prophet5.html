Prophet Dawud

The Mission of Dawud

Prophet Dawud (David, Peace Be Upon Him) was sent to guide the Israelites. He is known for his wisdom, leadership, and ability to judge fairly. Dawud was also a king, known for his devotion to Allah and his role in uniting the people. He received the Zabur (Psalms), a holy scripture from Allah, which emphasized devotion and praise of Allah.

The Battle with Goliath

One of the most famous stories of Prophet Dawud is his battle with the giant Goliath. Despite his youth, Dawud faced the mighty Goliath and defeated him with just a slingshot and faith in Allah. This victory marked the beginning of his leadership and reign as king.

His Wisdom and Justice

Prophet Dawud was known for his exceptional wisdom in resolving disputes and administering justice. His decisions are a model of fairness and integrity. Allah gave him the ability to understand the hearts of people, making his judgments highly respected and admired.
